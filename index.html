School Lunch Calendar

This page provides a calendar feed of school lunch menus that you can subscribe to in your favorite calendar application.

Subscribe to the Calendar

Use the following URL to subscribe to the lunch calendar:

https://YOUR-USERNAME.github.io/school-lunch-calendar/lunch_calendar.ics

How to Subscribe

Google Calendar

  1. Go to Google Calendar
  2. Click the "+" next to "Other calendars"
  3. Select "From URL"
  4. Paste the calendar URL and click "Add calendar"

Apple Calendar

  1. Open the Calendar app
  2. Go to File > New Calendar Subscription
  3. Paste the calendar URL and click "Subscribe"

Outlook

  1. Open Outlook
  2. Go to Calendar view
  3. Right-click "Other Calendars" and select "Add Calendar"
  4. Select "From Internet" and paste the calendar URL
